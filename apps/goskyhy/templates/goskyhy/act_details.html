Escape the City and Go to the Best Part

  • November 18, 2018
  • In San Francisco, CA, Golden Gate Park
  • 323 Comments

Currently we're averaging a 20 member turnout regardless of RSVP's however please do RSVP, especially if you're new to the group and or plan to play. NB: Regardless of event details and times our regulars still show up and play at various hours throughout the entire day. Feel free to post a message when in doubt.

This event will be officially a go ONLY-IF you have at least 4 total confirmed players no later than 11am before kick-off time (12:00 Noon), so that the rest of the confirmed players can go ahead with their day without much inconvenience if you don't have the players for a 2X2 play. However do feel free to post if anyone is there on our WhatsApp group. It is open to anyone who wants to rally-up players and host it. There are members who are willing to play on this Sunday event and many of us do live just minutes away. Feel free to suggest an edit for the time of the event at will, if you want. Remember, always any member can create and host an event and others will just jump-in. (tip: also announce it on the Whatsapp group)

The 1st player to get there should choose a men's court with antennas. Post the chosen court location (Row/Across) from the car park (Court #'s are confusing), or some form of identifying marker, eg umbrella, chair, towel, or bag color. All VB levels and friends are welcome. Bring a volleyball, water, towel, and perhaps some socks if its really hot out, etc.

Keep in contact with any informative incoming messages from players on your mobile app as the kickoff time draws near.

Free Parking: There is some free street parking along E Cabrillo Blvd overlooking the courts but it usually fills up quickly, there are also some at Ninos Dr, S Milpas St (a bit far though).

Paid Parking: There are couple of pay next to the courts & beach, the pay lot just east of the Cabrillo Bath House and just across from the Hyatt and cross street Ninos is your best bet.

The volleyball courts and picnic area are just east of the east lot.

There is a small bridge to the volleyball courts from the east parking lot.
